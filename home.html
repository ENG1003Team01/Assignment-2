Welcome!

Yesterday, you said tomorrow. Which means today, let's get running!

To start a new run, select 'Get Running!' from the menu bar on the left.

Once you Start running, you'll be able to monitor your speed, time lapsed and route taken. To finish your run, simply press Stop.

You'll be given the option to give your run a name and compare it to your previous runs over time.

If you'd just like to view your saved runs, select 'Run History'. Try to improve your runs each time.
